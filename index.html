What is Origami?

Origami is an ancient art based on simplicity: a sheet of paper, a fold, then another, and little by little, a shape comes to life. This minimalist art invites concentration and creativity, where every movement and every detail counts. Through origami, the essence of beauty is found in the simplicity of the gesture, the precision of the fold and the imagination that blossoms in the slightest curvature of the paper. It's not complexity that makes origami rich, but the ability to transform the ordinary into the extraordinary, with almost nothing.
